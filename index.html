FAQ’s

01

What is Wonderchat?

Wonderchat is a platform that allows you to instantly build an AI chatbot using your own knowledge base.
It is designed to help businesses provide self-serve customer support by automating responses based on
their specific data.

02

Does it support all languages?

The website indicates that the chatbot can be built to handle various user needs, and while the specific "all
languages" answer is behind a dropdown, the platform is designed for global business use with a 5-minute
setup process.

03

Do I need to know code to use Wonderchat?

No. The platform emphasizes a simple "5-minute set-up process" where you can create a chatbot just by
sharing a link or uploading a PDF file. It is built to be a user-friendly, self-serve tool.

04

Will I be able to embed the chatbot into my website?

Yes. The platform is designed for businesses to integrate the bot into their own systems to maximize support
capacity without extra hiring.

05

Can multiple team members in my organization manage my chatbots?

Yes. The provided dashboard view shows a comprehensive management interface designed for organizational
use, allowing businesses to oversee chatbot performance and data.
